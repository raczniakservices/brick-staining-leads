Overview

Raczniak Services provides lead capture pages, dashboards, and messaging automation for small businesses. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

Information we collect

  • Contact information submitted by users on lead forms (name, phone, email, address, details, photos if provided).
  • Messaging metadata needed to deliver and log SMS/voice automation (timestamps, sender/recipient numbers, message content when applicable).
  • Technical data (basic logs, IP address, user agent) for security and debugging.

How we use information

  • To deliver leads to the correct business and enable follow-up.
  • To send requested transactional messages (e.g., confirmations, routing, follow-up prompts).
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.

SMS / messaging privacy

We do not sell personal information. We do not share mobile information with third parties for marketing purposes. Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply.

Users can opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP.

Sharing

We share lead information with the business the user is contacting. We may use service providers (hosting, messaging providers) strictly to operate the system.

Data retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for operational purposes or as required by law. Retention periods may vary by client configuration.
